Add **Share of Voice** and **Opportunities** pages. Share of Voice shows how often AI engines mention you versus your tracked competitors, with a top-competitor donut and share-of-voice over time. Opportunities ranks non-branded prompts by the competitor-vs-you mention gap — biggest where competitors lead and you're absent — with an Opportunity Map scatter and a citation-stability spectrum. Prompts where no brand is mentioned are surfaced separately (nothing to win), and prompts you already lead are tagged "Won". A compact Share of Voice row is also added to the overview.
