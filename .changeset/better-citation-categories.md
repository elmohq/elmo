Revamp citation source categories and add a Google AI Mode breakdown.

Citations are now classified into Brand, Competitor, Editorial, Reviews, Social, PR, Reference, Institutional, Google, and Other — backed by a ~25k-domain editorial publisher list — surfacing earned-media and review-site coverage that previously fell into "Other". A new "Citations by Page Type" breakdown (article, listicle, how-to, comparison, product/pricing, docs, …) is inferred from each URL and title.

Google AI Mode Shopping product cards and search links are pulled out of the main citation mix into a dedicated module that shows which products Google recommends (split brand vs. competitor) and which searches it runs, each tied to the prompts that triggered them.
