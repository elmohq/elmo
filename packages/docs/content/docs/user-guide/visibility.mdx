## Visibility Page

The **Visibility** page shows per-prompt visibility scores and trends. This is where you get detailed insight into which prompts are driving AI mentions of your brand.

## Understanding Visibility Scores

For each prompt, Elmo shows:

- **Visibility score** — How prominently your brand appears in the AI response (0–100%)
- **Trend** — How the score has changed over recent evaluations
- **Last checked** — When the prompt was last sent to AI models

A high visibility score means AI models consistently mention your brand when answering that type of question. A low score suggests an opportunity to improve your content or SEO for that topic.

## Filtering and Sorting

You can filter prompts by tags and sort by score, trend direction, or recency. This helps you focus on:

- **Declining prompts** — Where you're losing visibility and may need to take action
- **Top performers** — Where your brand is already well-represented
- **New prompts** — Recently added prompts that haven't collected much data yet

## Per-Prompt Detail

Click on any prompt to see its full evaluation history, including:

- The exact responses from each AI model
- How the response changed over time
- Which competitors were mentioned in the same response
