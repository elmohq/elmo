## Overview

After creating your brand, Elmo launches the **Prompt Wizard** — an AI-powered tool that analyzes your website and generates relevant prompts for tracking. These prompts are the questions Elmo will ask AI models to monitor your brand's visibility.

## Wizard Steps

The wizard walks you through five stages:

### 1. Analyze Your Website

Elmo visits your website and extracts information about your products, services, and offerings.

{/* TODO: Screenshot of the website analysis step */}

### 2. Discover Keywords

Based on the analysis, Elmo generates relevant keywords that people might search for when looking for products or services like yours.

{/* TODO: Screenshot of the keywords step */}

You can review and adjust the suggested keywords before continuing.

### 3. Identify Competitors

Elmo suggests competitors based on your products and industry. These are the brands you want to track alongside your own in AI responses.

{/* TODO: Screenshot of the competitors step */}

Add, remove, or edit competitors as needed.

### 4. Define Personas

Elmo creates buyer personas that represent the types of people who would search for your products. Different personas may trigger different AI responses.

{/* TODO: Screenshot of the personas step */}

### 5. Generate Prompts

Combining keywords, competitors, and personas, Elmo generates a set of tracking prompts. Each prompt is a question that will be asked to AI models.

{/* TODO: Screenshot of the prompt generation step */}

Review the generated prompts and select the ones you want to track. You can also add custom prompts.

## After the Wizard

Once you confirm the prompts, Elmo marks your brand as onboarded and you're taken to the main dashboard. The worker will begin querying AI models on the next scheduled run.

You can always add, edit, or disable prompts later in **Settings → Prompts**.

## Manual Prompt Creation

If you prefer to skip the wizard or want to add prompts later, go to **Settings → Prompts** and add them manually. Each prompt needs:

- **Text** — The question to ask AI models
- **Tags** — Optional tags for filtering and organization
- **Enabled** — Toggle tracking on or off for individual prompts
