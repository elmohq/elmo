## Citations Page

The **Citations** page shows which URLs and domains AI models cite when answering prompts about your brand and industry.

## Citation Categories

Elmo categorizes citations into groups:

| Category | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Brand** | Citations pointing to your own domains |
| **Competitor** | Citations pointing to competitor domains |
| **Social Media** | Citations from social platforms (Reddit, Twitter, etc.) |
| **Google** | Citations from Google properties |
| **Institutional** | Citations from institutional sources (.edu, .gov, research) |

## Why Citations Matter

Citations tell you:

- **What sources AI models trust** for your industry — these are the pages you want to appear on or outrank
- **Where competitors are getting cited** — revealing content gaps or opportunities
- **Which of your pages AI models reference** — helping you understand what content performs well for AEO

## Using Citation Data

When you see competitors being cited more than your brand:

1. Identify the specific URLs being cited
2. Analyze what content those pages provide
3. Create or improve your own content to better answer those queries
4. Track whether your citation share improves over subsequent evaluations
