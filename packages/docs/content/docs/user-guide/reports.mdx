## Reports

Elmo can generate comprehensive AI visibility reports that summarize your brand's performance across all tracked prompts and AI models.

{/* TODO: Screenshot of the reports page */}

## Generating a Report

Reports are accessible from the sidebar under the **Reports** section (available to admin users). To generate a report:

1. Navigate to **Reports** in the sidebar
2. Click **Generate Report**
3. Select the date range and brands to include
4. Wait for the report to be generated

{/* TODO: Screenshot of the report generation flow */}

## Report Contents

A typical report includes:

- **Visibility summary** — Overall visibility score and trend
- **Per-prompt breakdown** — Visibility scores for each tracked prompt
- **Citation analysis** — Top cited sources and domain breakdown
- **Competitor comparison** — How your brand compares to tracked competitors
- **Recommendations** — Areas where visibility can be improved

## Sharing Reports

Generated reports can be viewed directly in the browser via a shareable URL. This makes it easy to share results with team members or stakeholders who don't have an Elmo account.
